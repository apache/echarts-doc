Mailing List

About Mailing List

Mailing list is where we discuss in public and keep everything tracked. You are welcomed to subscribe it if you wish:

  • To be informed about bug reports or feature requests;
  • To discuss about developing plans or specific issues;
  • To offer helps to those who ask questions by email;
  • And etc.

If you have a specific bug to report or feature request, we'd suggest you opening an issue with our issue helper tool, which is a more efficient way to report the details.

commits@echarts.incubator.apache.org focuses on the commit logs, while dev@echarts.incubator.apache.org holds other general discussions.

These two are public mailing list, and you can get access to them on Website https://lists.apache.org/list.html?commits@echarts.apache.org and https://lists.apache.org/list.html?dev@echarts.apache.org without subscribing.

How To Subscribe

Email commits-subscribe@echarts.incubator.apache.org or dev-subscribe@echarts.incubator.apache.org to subscribe commits@echarts.incubator.apache.org and dev@echarts.incubator.apache.org accordingly.

You should receive an email and please follow the instructions in that.
